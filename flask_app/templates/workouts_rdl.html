Kettlebell RDL

    Notes for Kettlebell RDL:

  • ✅ Maintain a flat back posture.
  • ✅ Unlock or slightly bend your knees to start.
  • ✅ Shoot your hips posterior.
  • ✅ Allow the weight to drop straight toward the ground.
  • ✅ Use your glutes and hamstrings to pull back to the starting position.
  • ❌ DO NOT round your back to try and reach the ground
